# 🔐 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

## ✅ General Online Safety Tips
1. **Use strong and unique passwords**  
   Create passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using the same password across multiple accounts.

2. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**  
   Always turn on 2FA for email, social media, banking, and academic accounts.

3. **Beware of phishing links and emails**  
   Do not click on suspicious links or download unknown attachments, even if they appear to be from trusted sources.

4. **Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions**  
   Public networks are insecure—avoid logging into banking or personal accounts on them.

5. **Keep your devices updated**  
   Regularly update your phone, laptop, and apps to fix security vulnerabilities.

6. **Limit personal information sharing online**  
   Avoid posting sensitive details like phone number, address, or college ID publicly.

---

## 💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
7. **Never share your UPI PIN or OTP**  
   No bank or payment app will ever ask for your PIN or OTP—sharing it can lead to instant fraud.

8. **Verify payment requests before approving**  
   Always double-check the sender’s name and purpose before approving any UPI collect request.

9. **Use trusted apps only**  
   Install payment apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

10. **Check transaction alerts regularly**  
   Enable SMS/email alerts and immediately report any unauthorized transaction.

---

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
11. **Report incidents immediately**  
   If you face any cyber fraud or suspicious activity, report it without delay.

12. **Use official reporting channels**  
   - 🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
   - 📞 Helpline Number: 1930

---

## ⚠️ Final Reminder
Stay alert, think before you click, and always verify before you trust. Your digital safety is in your hands!
