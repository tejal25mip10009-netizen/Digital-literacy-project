Digital Literacy Infographic Report
I created my infographic using Canva, a user-friendly design tool that provides various templates
and visual elements. My design focuses on explaining the concept of digital literacy and its
importance for students in today’s digital world. The infographic includes key topics such as the
meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, email etiquette,
and maintaining a professional online presence. Through this design, I aimed to make the
information simple, visually appealing, and easy to understand. I used icons, headings, and short
bullet points to ensure clarity and engagement. The infographic highlights how digital literacy helps
students stay safe online, improve their learning, and build a strong digital identity. One challenge I
faced while creating the infographic was organizing all the information in a single page without
making it look crowded. However, I overcame this by using proper spacing, color schemes, and
minimal text. Overall, this task helped me understand the importance of digital literacy and
improved my design skills
