Social Media Checklist (social-media-checklist.md)
✅ Do’s
Think before posting content
Maintain a respectful and polite tone
Verify information before sharing
Protect your personal information
Use social media for learning and networking


❌ Don’ts
Do not share fake or misleading information
Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
Do not engage in online arguments or trolling
Avoid oversharing personal details
Do not misuse someone else’s content without credit
